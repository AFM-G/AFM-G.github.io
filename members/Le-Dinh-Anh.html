Position: Principal Investigator

Research Interests

  • Cavitation
  • Renewable Energy - Wind Turbines

Short Bio

Assoc. Prof. Le Dinh Anh is the principal investigator of the Applied Fluid Mechanics Research Group. He received the MSc and PhD degrees from Tohoku University, Japan in the field of Fluid Mechanics. His researches are focusing on cavitation-multiphase flow, renewable ennergy - wind/hydrokinetic turbines, and hydro–aerodynamic optimization using CFD and EFD techniques
